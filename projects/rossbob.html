RossBob - A painting robot

Info: A 24h hackathon project I worked with Dominic Peel, Martin Lombard, and Kobi Kelemen on.

Problem: A common criticism of image-gen software is that it isn't "real" like human-made art.

Solution: A software worksflow that transforms images from Dalle-3 into brush-stroke instructions for human-like painting by a 6DoF robot.
