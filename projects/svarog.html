Interstellar CubeSat : Svarog

Info: I led the experiment design team of Project Svarog - publishing in the Journal of the British Interplanetary Society. Project Svarog is a student-run initiative of the Imperial College Space Society. Inspired by Oumuamua during my first term as Chair/President - how might we (as a student engineering group) send an object beyond our solar system?

Problem: How might we (a student society) launch an object into Interstellar space within a reasonable time frame?

Solution: A 3U CubeSat with a solar sail released on a trans-martian trajectory.
